28/05/2017 - Cafe Rose


Today's event was focused on rock music. On our stage, made available to us by owners of Cafe Rose, eight artists presented themselves to our audience. They played old and new songs. After the concert the participants had the opportunity to talk to artists or ask them for autographs.

We had the opportunity to listen to very rock songs as well as calm atmospheric ballads. Thus, every listener had the opportunity to find something for yourself.

Besides, the participants of the event could also try local delicacies prepared for us by Cafe Rose, as well as drink excellent home-made wine or various types of beers from the local brewery.

The event has been great. All this was possible only thanks to you. We cordially invite you to the next event.
